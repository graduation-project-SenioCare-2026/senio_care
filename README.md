---

## 📌 Overview

**SenioCare** is a cross-platform healthcare mobile application developed to improve elderly care management by connecting elderly users, caregivers, and healthcare service providers in a unified digital ecosystem.

The application provides intelligent healthcare assistance, medication management, emergency support, health monitoring, and AI-driven insights to enhance safety, independence, and quality of life for elderly users.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Improve healthcare accessibility for elderly users
- Support medication adherence through smart reminders
- Enable caregivers to monitor elderly health remotely
- Provide AI-based healthcare assistance and reports
- Create a communication bridge between elderly users and service providers

---

# 👥 User Roles

## 👴 Elder

The Elder module focuses on providing a simple and secure healthcare experience.

### Main Capabilities

- Personalized onboarding process
- Medical profile creation
- Chronic diseases and allergy management
- Medication scheduling and reminders
- Medical records management
- AI medical chat assistance
- AI-generated health reports
- Healthcare service requests
- Emergency SOS assistance
- Profile management


---

## 👩‍⚕️ Caregiver

The Caregiver module enables remote elderly monitoring and support.

### Main Capabilities

- Elder profile management
- Health information monitoring
- Medication adherence tracking
- Health statistics visualization
- Multi-elder management
- Emergency SOS alerts


---

## 🏥 Service Provider

The Service Provider module allows healthcare providers to publish and manage services.

### Main Capabilities

- Service creation and management
- Update service information
- Remove unavailable services
- Display available services
- Provider profile management


---

# 🤖 AI Features

## AI Medical Chat Assistant

Provides conversational healthcare support by:

- Answering general health questions
- Explaining medical information
- Providing basic healthcare guidance


## AI Medical Report Generation

Generates personalized health reports based on user data.

The AI system analyzes:

- Medical profile
- Health records
- Medication information

Provides:

- Health summaries
- Risk indicators
- Personalized recommendations
- Lifestyle suggestions
